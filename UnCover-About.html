About

What is Uncover

Uncover is a digital book swapping service that helps connect book owners
who are interested in trading their used books with others.
It's a win-win because everyone gets to enjoy a new story without needing to spend the extra cash.
Another bonus is that it helps to create more space at home to store old books.
But there is a catch! Unlike the titles or covers you can see above, the trick is that the titles and covers are hidden,
so what you get in the mail is a mystery!

How it works

It's easy in these 5 steps

  1. List books you'd like to swap with other club members.
  2. Once a book is requested, mail it to the individual who requested it. You pay postage for the books you send out
  3. In return, you can choose from lots of other available books! The books you receive come to you postage-paid.
  4. Finally, the books you request are yours to keep or swap again!
