What is Uncover?


Uncover is a digital book swapping service that connects book owners in your town.
Are you interested in trading their books to avoid buying new ones?

You're in the right place - UnCover offers members a win-win because everyone
can enjoy a new book without paying full price or having to make more space at home to store old books.

One more unique characteristic: the titles and covers are hidden, so what you receive in the post is a mystery!



How it works

It's easy using these 5 steps!

  1. List books you'd like to swap with other club members.
  2. Once a book is requested, post it to the club member. You pay postage for the books you send out
  3. In return, you can choose from 1,511,933 available books! The books you receive come to you postage-paid.
  4. Books you request are yours to keep, or swap again!
